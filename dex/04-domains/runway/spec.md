# Runway Domain

**Financial scaffolding and resilience.**

Runway measures how long you can survive without income.

---

## Domain Purpose

Runway hardening reduces fragility by:

1. **Extending time buffer** — from days to weeks to months  
2. **Reducing burn rate** — cutting unnecessary recurring costs  
3. **Diversifying reserves** — cash, assets, credit lines  
4. **Automating safety nets** — auto-transfers, bill-pay, emergency protocols  

---

## Core Metric

**Runway = liquid reserves ÷ monthly burn rate**

**Example:**  
- Liquid reserves: $9,000  
- Monthly burn: $3,000  
- **Runway: 3 months**  

---

## Node Categories

### 1. Emergency Fund Nodes

- **1-month emergency fund** (Secured: $3K, Hardened: $6K)  
- **3-month emergency fund** (Secured: $9K, Hardened: $15K)  
- **6-month emergency fund** (Secured: $18K, Hardened: $30K)  

### 2. Burn Reduction Nodes

- **Fixed-rate refinance** (cuts monthly burn by 10–15%)  
- **Subscription audit** (eliminate unused recurring costs)  
- **Housing cost optimization** (roommate, downsize, relocate)  
- **Transport cost reduction** (bike, transit pass, carpool)  

### 3. Reserve Diversification Nodes

- **High-yield savings account** (4–5% APY)  
- **Credit line established** (backup liquidity)  
- **Asset liquidity** (sellable gear, crypto, investments)  
- **Backup currency** (USD, EUR, stablecoins)  

### 4. Automation Nodes

- **Auto-transfer to savings** (10–20% of income)  
- **Bill autopay enabled** (rent, utilities, insurance)  
- **Emergency protocol documented** (what to do if income stops)

## Objectives (Sample)

### Easy

1. **Calculate monthly burn rate**  
   - Track all expenses for 30 days  
   - Sum recurring costs  
   - Divide by 30 for daily burn  

2. **Open high-yield savings account**  
   - Research options (Wise, Revolut, local banks)  
   - Open account  
   - Transfer $100 as initial deposit  

3. **Cancel unused subscription**  
   - Audit all recurring charges  
   - Cancel at least one unused service  
   - Confirm cancellation  

### Medium

1. **Build 1-month emergency fund**  
   - Save $3,000 (or local equivalent)  
   - Store in high-yield savings  
   - Do not touch for 30 days  

2. **Set up auto-transfer to savings**  
   - Configure 10% of income to auto-transfer  
   - Test for one month  
   - Confirm consistency  

3. **Reduce monthly burn by 10%**  
   - Identify largest expense category  
   - Negotiate, optimize, or eliminate  
   - Confirm reduction over 60 days  

### Hard

1. **Build 3-month emergency fund**  
   - Save $9,000 (or local equivalent)  
   - Maintain for 90 days without dipping  
   - Document emergency protocol  

2. **Secure fixed-rate refinance**  
   - Negotiate lower interest rate on debt  
   - Cut monthly burn by 15%  
   - Lock rate for 12+ months  

3. **Establish backup credit line**  
   - Apply for $5K+ credit line  
   - Keep unused (emergency only)  
   - Document access protocol  

---

## Ladders (Disproportionate Gains)

**Examples:**  
- Fixed-rate refinance reduces burn by 15% → instant Runway extension  
- Windfall income (tax refund, bonus) → emergency fund jumps from 1 month to 3 months  
- Housing cost cut by 30% (roommate, relocation) → Runway doubles  

**Effect:**  
- Jump multiple node states at once  
- Runway Domain Ascent boost  
- Achievement unlock ("Three-Month Creature")  

---

## Hazards (Regressions)

**Examples:**  
- Surprise repair ($800) → emergency fund dips below threshold  
- Income loss (client drops) → burn rate unchanged, reserves deplete  
- Medical bill → emergency fund depleted  
- Rent increase → monthly burn goes up, Runway shortens  

**Effect:**  
- Node drops from Hardened → Secured or Secured → Active  
- Runway Domain Ascent position drops  
- Threat Console alert triggered

**Node states:**  
- 1-month fund Secured: 10 pts

- 3-month fund Secured: 20 pts  
- 3-month fund Hardened: 60 pts  
- 6-month fund Secured: 40 pts  
- 6-month fund Hardened: 120 pts  

**Objectives:**  
- Easy: +5 pts  
- Medium: +10 pts  
- Hard: +20 pts  

**Milestones:**  
- Full Runway domain secured (all nodes secured): +50 pts  
- Full Runway domain hardened (all nodes hardened): +150 pts  

**Ladders:**  
- Each ladder event: +20 pts  

**Hazards survived:**  
- Survived without regression: +10 pts
